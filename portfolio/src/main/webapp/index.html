Vanderbilt Effective Altruism

My friend Anjay and I started Vanderbilt Effective Altruism in 2019 with the aim of spreading knowledge of how we can do the most good we can among our university. We ran the arete fellowship, a one hour a week introduction to effective altruism. 24 students from Vanderbilt participated. In total fellows pledge approximately $930,000 of their future income to effective charities and more than half decided to pursue a career of doing good.
